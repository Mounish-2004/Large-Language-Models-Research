# Large Language Models (LLMs) Research

## Overview
A research-based study on modern Large Language Models including ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and DeepSeek.

## Topics Covered
- Transformer architecture
- Pre-training & Fine-tuning
- Applications of LLMs
- Limitations
- Future potential

## Key Learnings
- Understanding of transformer-based models
- Multimodal AI systems
- AI alignment and ethical challenges
